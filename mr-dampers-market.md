<p><strong>The MR Dampers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Damper</li><li>Semi Active Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MR dampers, or Magnetorheological dampers, are classified into active and semi-active types based on their control mechanisms. Active dampers provide real-time adjustments to the damping force using external power and sensors, allowing for precise control of vehicle stability and comfort. In contrast, semi-active dampers adjust the fluid's viscosity using magnetic fields without external power, offering a balance between performance and efficiency. Both types enhance ride quality and safety in various applications, including automotive and structural engineering.</p></p>
