<p><ul><li>Gear Hydraulic Starter</li><li>Piston Hydraulic Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic starter market is primarily divided into two types: gear hydraulic starters and piston hydraulic starters. Gear hydraulic starters utilize gears to convert hydraulic pressure into rotational motion, ideal for applications requiring high torque. In contrast, piston hydraulic starters employ a piston mechanism to generate the necessary pressure, offering efficient starting in heavy-duty engines. Both types serve critical functions in various industries, including aviation, marine, and industrial machinery, catering to specific engine sizes and operational requirements.</p></p>
