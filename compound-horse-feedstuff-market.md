<p><strong>The Compound Horse Feedstuff Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pellets</li><li>Crumbles/Cubes</li><li>Powder/Mash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compound horse feedstuff market comprises three main types: pellets, crumbles/cubes, and powder/mash. Pellets are compacted feeds that provide a balanced nutrient profile and are easy to store and handle. Crumbles or cubes offer similar nutritional value but have a coarser texture, appealing to horses that prefer chewing. Powder/mash is a finely ground feed that can be mixed with water to create a soft, palatable feed option, ideal for horses requiring easier digestion or with dental issues.</p></p>
