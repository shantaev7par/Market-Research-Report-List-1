<p><strong>The Fireproofing Coatings for Wood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fireproofing coatings for wood are categorized primarily into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based fireproofing coatings are environmentally friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and easy to apply, making them a popular choice for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings offer enhanced durability and may provide better performance in extreme conditions, but they often contain higher VOC levels. Both types effectively impede combustion and protect wooden structures from fire damage, catering to various project requirements.</p></p>
