<p><p>The environmentally friendly paper and plastic market includes two primary production methods: dry pressing and wet pressing. In dry pressing, raw materials are compressed without the use of water, resulting in lower energy consumption and reduced environmental impact. Conversely, wet pressing involves using water during the production process, which aids in fiber bonding but may lead to higher water use and waste. Both methods aim to promote sustainability by minimizing harmful chemicals and enhancing recyclability.</p></p>
