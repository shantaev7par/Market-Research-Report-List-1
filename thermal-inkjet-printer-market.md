<p><p>The Thermal Inkjet Printer market is divided into two primary types: Roof Shooter and Side Shooter. Roof Shooter Thermal Inkjet Printers utilize a vertical printing mechanism, where ink is ejected from nozzles positioned above the substrate, allowing for efficient space management and streamlined design. Conversely, Side Shooter Thermal Inkjet Printers feature nozzles aligned horizontally, which can improve print quality and consistency on various surfaces, making them suitable for diverse applications. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, enhancing productivity and print precision.</p></p>
