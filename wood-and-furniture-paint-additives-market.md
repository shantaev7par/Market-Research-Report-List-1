<p><strong>The Wood and Furniture Paint Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood and furniture paint additives market is primarily categorized into solventborne and waterborne types. Solventborne additives utilize organic solvents, offering durability and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for exterior applications. They often provide a smoother finish but emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, waterborne additives use water as the primary solvent, resulting in lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup. These are increasingly favored for indoor applications due to their environmental benefits and rapid drying times, promoting safer usage.</p></p>
