<p><p>The Automotive Battery Management Controller (BMC) market encompasses two main types: Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV) BMC and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) BMC. BEV BMCs are designed for fully electric vehicles, managing energy storage systems to optimize battery performance, safety, and longevity. In contrast, PHEV BMCs cater to hybrid vehicles, overseeing dual energy sources—electric and internal combustion—ensuring efficient energy distribution and charge management. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance and improving energy efficiency.</p></p>
