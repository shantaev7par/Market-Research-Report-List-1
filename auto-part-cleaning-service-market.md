<p><p>The auto part cleaning service market primarily comprises two types: water-based cleaning and solvent-based cleaning. Water-based cleaning utilizes aqueous solutions, often incorporating detergents and surfactants, to effectively remove dirt, grease, and contaminants while being environmentally friendly. In contrast, solvent-based cleaning employs organic solvents to dissolve and remove stubborn residues, providing a more aggressive approach for heavy-duty cleaning tasks. Each method has its advantages, catering to varying cleanliness requirements and environmental considerations in the automotive maintenance industry.</p></p>
