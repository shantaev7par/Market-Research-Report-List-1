<p><p>Non-invasive Photoacoustic Imaging (NPAI) is an advanced imaging technique that combines the principles of optical excitation and ultrasound detection to visualize tissues in real-time. This method leverages the absorption of light by biological tissues, creating localized thermal expansion that generates ultrasound waves. NPAI is particularly valuable in medical diagnostics as it provides detailed information about tissue composition, vascular structures, and the presence of tumors without the need for invasive procedures.</p><p>The Non-invasive Photoacoustic Imaging Market is anticipated to grow significantly, with an expected CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
