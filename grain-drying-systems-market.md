<p><p>The grain drying systems market comprises two main types: stationary and mobile grain dryers. Stationary grain dryers are fixed installations typically used in larger operations, offering high capacity and efficiency for processing significant volumes of grain. In contrast, mobile grain dryers are portable units designed for flexibility, allowing farmers to dry grain on-site and adapt to varying crop conditions. Both systems aim to preserve grain quality, reduce spoilage, and improve overall storage efficiency, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
