<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter market includes two primary types: straight catheters and pigtail-curled catheters. Straight catheters are designed for straightforward insertion and efficient fluid exchange, offering a simple structure for access to the peritoneal cavity. In contrast, pigtail-curled catheters feature a curved design that helps secure the catheter in place and reduces the risk of displacement. Both types serve essential roles in peritoneal dialysis, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
