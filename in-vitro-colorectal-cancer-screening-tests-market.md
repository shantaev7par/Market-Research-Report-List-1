<p><p>The in-vitro colorectal cancer screening tests market comprises several types, including biomarker tests, fecal occult blood tests (FOBT), and CRC DNA screening tests. Biomarker tests identify specific biological markers associated with cancer, aiding in early diagnosis. FOBTs detect hidden blood in stool samples, indicating possible cancer presence. CRC DNA screening tests analyze genetic material in stool for mutations linked to colorectal cancer, providing a non-invasive detection method. Together, these tests enhance early detection and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
