<p><p>The currency detector market encompasses several technologies, including optical sensing, GMR sensor proximity detection, and physical attributes detection. Optical sensing utilizes light to identify and verify banknotes, while GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) sensors detect magnetic properties in currency for authenticity. Proximity detection combines various sensing methods to ensure accurate recognition when bills are near the device. Physical attributes detection evaluates features such as texture, size, and color, enhancing overall reliability in currency validation and counterfeiting prevention.</p></p>
