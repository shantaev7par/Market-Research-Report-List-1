<p><strong>The Dedicated Hybrid Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVT-based</li><li>DCT-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dedicated Hybrid Transmission market includes two primary types: CVT-based (Continuously Variable Transmission) and DCT-based (Dual-Clutch Transmission). CVT-based systems offer smooth acceleration and optimal fuel efficiency by seamlessly adjusting gear ratios, making them ideal for city driving. In contrast, DCT-based systems use two separate clutches for quick gear shifts, enhancing performance and responsiveness, particularly in sporty driving conditions. Both types cater to the growing demand for hybrid vehicles, balancing efficiency and driving experience.</p></p>
