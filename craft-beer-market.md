<p><strong>The Craft Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ales</li><li>Lagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The craft beer market is primarily divided into two main types: ales and lagers. Ales are fermented at warmer temperatures, resulting in a rich, fruity flavor profile and often have higher alcohol content. Common varieties include IPAs, stouts, and porters. Lagers, on the other hand, are fermented at cooler temperatures, producing a crisp and clean taste, with popular types like pilsners and helles. Both categories appeal to diverse consumer preferences, driving growth in the craft beer segment.</p></p>
